Welcome

Hi, welcome to my personal website!

Frank Kalungi is a research scientist passionate about artificial intelligence and biotechnology. As an independent developer, I build web, mobile, and desktop applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, Kivy, and Tkinter.

At the National Agricultural Research Laboratories (NARL) in Kawanda, Uganda, I work as a genome engineer. I developed the first gene-edited East African Highland bananas using CRISPR/Cas9, differential gene expression, molecular cloning, HPLC analysis, and microbiology.

About Me

I am passionate about technology and its potential to revolutionize humanity's future. As a self-taught programmer with over 10 years of experience, I specialize in designing web, mobile, and desktop software. I am particularly drawn to applying programming to biological sciences to accelerate drug, vaccine, and disease-resistant crop development.

Through technology, I aim to contribute to Africa's growing biotechnology industry by developing bioinformatics software and pipelines for drug design and genome engineering.
